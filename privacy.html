Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026
Your Privacy Matters: We take your privacy seriously. All audio processing happens locally on your device—your audio never leaves your Mac.

1. What Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information:

  • Email Address: Required for trial activation and license management
  • License Information: License key and activation/expiry dates for verification

Technical Information

We may collect:

  • Last license verification timestamp (for offline support)
  • macOS version (for compatibility purposes)
  • Error logs (only if you explicitly share them)
  • License status (trial vs. paid)

Audio Data

We DO NOT collect, process, or store any audio data. All audio processing happens locally on your device using on-device AI models. Your microphone input never leaves your computer.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for:

  • Activating and managing your trial or purchased license
  • Verifying license validity during online checks
  • Sending important updates about your license or account
  • Providing customer support if you contact us
  • Improving the Software (anonymized usage patterns only)

3. Offline Operation

Clarify is designed to work offline. After you activate your license online, you can use Clarify for up to 30 days without an internet connection. During this time:

  • Your local license file is used for verification
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Audio processing continues to work normally
  • When you reconnect, we verify your license status

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All server communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Your license file is stored securely in your macOS Keychain
  • We use database encryption for sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No storage of passwords or payment information (handled by payment processors)

5. Third-Party Services

Clarify integrates with:

  • Payment Processor: Stripe handles checkout and payment. Stripe has its own privacy policy.
  • Analytics: We may use anonymized analytics to understand feature usage. No personal identifiers are included.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Payment Processors: As necessary for payment processing
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: Only contractors bound by confidentiality agreements

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request what personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@clarifyaudio.com

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Session management (login, preferences)
  • Analytics (understanding how visitors use the site)
  • Security (CSRF protection)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit site functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Clarify is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

10. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

For EU Residents (GDPR): We process personal data lawfully based on your consent (license activation). You have all rights under GDPR.

For California Residents (CCPA): You have rights to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information. Contact privacy@clarifyaudio.com for requests.

11. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your license is active, plus
  • 90 days after a refund (for support purposes)
  • Longer if required by law

You can request deletion of your account at any time.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We'll notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Requiring your consent for significant changes

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

Email: privacy@clarifyaudio.com
Website: clarifyaudio.com
Mailing Address: Clarify Audio, Inc., United States

14. California Privacy Rights Summary

Under California law, you have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information is collected, used, shared
  • Delete: Your personal information (with exceptions)
  • Opt-Out: The sale or sharing of personal information
  • Non-Discrimination: You won't be discriminated against for exercising these rights
